MMGCS is located at Airport West in Accra, the capital city of Ghana

The prestigious Mary Mother of Good Counsel School was established in 1998 with the mission of delivering quality holistic education with a commitment to moral, ethical and spiritual development based on Catholic Christian principles to pupils from all nationalities and religious backgrounds.

The school's motto: Knowledge, Service and Humility, charges its pupils to acquire knowledge for the service of mankind in humility.
